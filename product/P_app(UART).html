General Description

JBT24M module is a low energy , low cost, and high performance Smart wireless transceiver module based on TI CC2540/CC2541 Single-Chip solution.

The JBT24M module runs both Application and Smart protocol Stack, includes peripheral interfaces with various sensors and controls; suitable for Smart Phone enabled wireless sensor & control applications such as security & alarm, remote data sensing & acquisition, healthcare monitor, industrial control, etc.

JBT24M can widely be applied to data transmitting, signal control and those specified as preferred components in remote control garage, roller curtain, door locks, remote sensing, telemetry, industrial control and wireless security alarm industry.



Applications

  • Mobile Phone Accessories
  • Wireless data communication
  • Remote control
  • Home automation & security
  • Health Care and Medical
  • Industrial Control & Data Acquisition
  • Personal Alarm Systems
  • Wireless toy and game control
  • LED Lighting Control
  • Consumer electronic


    • Features

  • Operating Frequency: 2400MHz ~ 2483.5 MHz ISM Band.
  • Supply Voltage: 2Vdc – 3.6Vdc.
  • 2.4-GHz Bluetooth Smart technology Compliant.
  • Supports 250-kbps, 500-kbps, 1-Mbps & 2-Mbps Data Rates..
  • Suitable for Systems Targeting Compliance With World Radio Frequency Regulation: ETSI EN 300 328 and EN 300 440 Class 2(Europe), FCC CFR47 Part 15(US), ARIB STD-T66(Japan) .
