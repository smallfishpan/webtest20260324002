Features

Key Features
  • Listen to worldwide internet radio stations (more than 10,000 stations!).
  • Configure the internet connectivity via wired ethernet or wireless LAN(WiFi).
  • Maintain up to three different customized network configurations.
  • Operate the Radio through Intuitive Front Panel Control or through full featured Remote Control.
  • Preset 4 quick stations on the radio Front Panel or 10 quick stations on the Remote Control.
  • Front panel Power On to the last station listened instantly, no need to re-tune the radio channel.
  • Update radio station list automatically from vTuner.
  • Add customized 20 more favorite radio stations play list from vTuner.
  • Support UPnP client for listening music stored on UPnP media server
  • Support English, French , German , Italian , and Spanish language selection.
